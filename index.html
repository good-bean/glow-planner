Bridget's Glow Plan

A bold and clear roadmap to strength and softness

Weekly Workout Split

  • Day 1 – Glutes & Core Sculpt
  • Day 2 – Feminine Pilates Flow
  • Day 3 – Rest / Light Stretch
  • Day 4 – Full Body + Row
  • Day 5 – Glutes + Cardio Intervals
  • Days 6 & 7 – Gentle Yoga or Rest

4-Week Indoor Feminine Physique Plan

Repeat each weekly split for 4 weeks. Progress by increasing reps or intensity each week.

Day 1 – Glutes & Core Sculpt

  • Glute Bridges – 15 reps + 10 pulses
  • Fire Hydrants – 15 per side
  • Side-Lying Leg Lifts – 15 per side
  • Forearm Plank with Hip Dips – 30 sec
  • Dead Bug – 12 reps
  • Wall Sit – 45 sec

Day 2 – Feminine Pilates Flow

  • Roll Downs – 5 slow reps
  • Leg Circles – 10 each direction
  • Single Leg Stretch – 10 per side
  • Clamshells – 15 per side
  • Side Plank Knee Tap – 10 per side
  • Glute Bridge Hold – 30 sec + 20 pulses
  • Child’s Pose to Cobra Stretch – 1 min

Day 4 – Full Body + Row

  • Squats – 15 reps
  • Push-Ups (Incline if needed) – 10-12
  • Glute Kickbacks – 15 per leg
  • Bird-Dog – 12 per side
  • Wall Sit – 45 sec
  • Side-Lying Leg Raise – 15 per side
  • Rowing Finisher: 4x 30 sec fast / 90 sec rest

Day 5 – Glutes + Row Intervals

  • Donkey Kicks – 15 per leg
  • Glute Bridge March – 12 reps
  • Fire Hydrants – 20 per side
  • Lying Leg Raises – 15
  • Clamshell + Pulse – 10 + 10 pulses
  • Row Intervals: 6x 1 min moderate / 1 min rest

Daily Check-In

  • Moved my body today
  • Drank 2+ liters of water
  • Ate ~1,900–2,000 calories
  • Took time to breathe or stretch
  • Gave myself kindness
